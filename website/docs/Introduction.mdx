## ICE thinks this is a game.
Since the start of 2025, the United States Department of Homeland Security have used advertisements based on popular video games such as Pokémon and Halo [1][2].
These ads portray immigrants as unruly animals and mindless infected whose only purpose is to suffer for their amusement.
Although broadly condemned by their franchisors, these advertisements still served their purpose. In the months since such campaigns ran, 
DHS has more than doubled the number of ICE agents currently tenured [3].

And with the advent of Operation Metro surge, thousands of these agents have flooded our streets, each one looking for their next hit of violence.

## Softlocking
They can try invade our cities, but the game stops here. 
Communities across the United States have organized en masse to ensure that these hits never come, 
[softlocking](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/softlock) entire cities to prevent our neighbors from being kidnapped [5][6].
